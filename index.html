IHD Mastering

A structured curriculum for Ischemic Heart Disease evaluation, diagnosis, and management.

1

Foundations & Pathophysiology

Atherosclerosis formation, endothelial dysfunction, coronary anatomy, and the ischemic cascade.
2

Chronic Coronary Syndromes (CCS)

Stable angina, risk stratification, non-invasive testing (stress testing, CTA), and long-term medical therapy.
3

Acute Coronary Syndromes (ACS)

Unstable angina and NSTEMI. Plaque rupture mechanisms, risk scores (TIMI, GRACE), and acute pharmacological management.
4

Myocardial Infarction (MI)

STEMI criteria, reperfusion timelines (PCI vs. Fibrinolysis), mechanical complications, and cardiogenic shock.
5

Secondary Prevention & Follow-up

Post-MI lipid management, DAPT duration, beta-blockers, ACE inhibitors, and cardiac rehabilitation.
